Layers are the sounds playing at once. The dot turns each on or off; the slider is its volume.

Drone the held chords · Bass the low foundation · Shimmer a sparkle cloud · Bells stray high notes · Sub a slow heartbeat · Air a faint texture bed · Light an airy synth.

Field shapes the whole feel. Movement how often notes appear · Space reverb size · Tape warmth + hiss · Age tape wear, darker and noisier the higher it goes (the Basinski move) · Volume loudness · Tide lets it slowly evolve on its own.

The Bowl / Cathedral chips up top are the instrument. + Load sound (or drag a file in) turns any audio on your drive into a new tape-looped instrument.
